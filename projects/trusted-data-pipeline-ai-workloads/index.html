Trusted Data Pipeline for AI Workloads

Fabric-oriented pipeline pattern for turning operational data into trusted tables, curated embeddings, and observable downstream AI workflows.

Problem

AI systems inherit the quality, lineage, access, and observability problems of the data products that feed them. This project focuses on making those foundations explicit.

Delivery Focus

  • Curated tables and reusable data products for analytics and AI
  • Embedding preparation with quality and privacy checks
  • Operational monitoring for downstream assistant and RAG workflows

Architecture

01 Operational sources

Structured and semi-structured data sources are profiled before downstream use.

02 Fabric pipeline

Transformations produce trusted tables, validation outputs, and reusable data products.

03 Embedding layer

Curated content is prepared for retrieval with metadata and privacy-aware controls.

04 AI workflow

Assistants and analytics tools consume governed data with monitoring and lineage context.
