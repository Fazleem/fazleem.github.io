Public-Sector Document AI Assistant

RAG assistant concept for policy, regulatory, and program documents where answers need citations, traceability, and audit-ready retrieval behavior.

Problem

Public-sector teams often manage large policy and program document collections where staff need fast answers, but answers must remain grounded in source material.

Delivery Focus

  • Document ingestion and metadata-aware chunking
  • Semantic retrieval with source citations
  • Review trails for prompts, retrieved passages, and generated answers

Architecture

01 Document intake

Policies, procedures, program guides, and regulatory documents are normalized for retrieval.

02 Semantic index

Chunks, metadata, and embeddings support filtered search and source-grounded context.

03 Cited response

The assistant returns answers with source references and confidence-aware language.

04 Review trail

Prompts, retrieved sources, generated answers, and reviewer notes remain auditable.
